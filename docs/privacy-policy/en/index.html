Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

CourtLens (the "App") is an iOS application for recording tennis and soft tennis match/practice sessions, logging point data, and generating statistics and coaching insights. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

All data is stored exclusively on your device. The App does not transmit any data to external servers.

  • Profile information: Name, dominant hand, play style, sport type (entered voluntarily)
  • Session records: Match/practice records and point logs (shot type, result, court position, etc.)
  • Video file references: URI of videos selected or recorded via camera (video files are never transmitted externally)

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Crash reports (via external services)
  • Analytics or behavioral data (via external services)
  • Payment information

4. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to display statistics and generate analysis reports within the App. It is never shared with third parties.

5. Camera, Microphone, and Photo Library Access

PermissionPurpose
CameraTo record video of matches and practice sessions
MicrophoneTo capture audio during video recording
Photo LibraryTo select previously recorded videos from your library

These permissions are only used when explicitly granted. Video data is never transmitted externally.

6. Data Storage and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App or using the "Reset Data" function in Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ but does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Significant changes will be communicated through App update release notes.

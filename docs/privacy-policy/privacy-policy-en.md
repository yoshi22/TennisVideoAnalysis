# CourtLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CourtLens (the "App") is an iOS application for recording tennis and soft tennis match/practice sessions, logging point data, and generating statistics and coaching insights. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

## 2. Information We Collect

All data is stored exclusively on your device. The App does not transmit any data to external servers.

Information used by the App:

- **Profile information**: Name, dominant hand, play style, sport type (entered voluntarily by the user)
- **Session records**: Match/practice records and point logs (shot type, result, court position, etc.)
- **Video file references**: URI of videos selected from your library or recorded via camera (video files are never transmitted externally)

All of the above is stored locally on your device only.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

- Location data
- Contacts
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Crash reports (via external services)
- Analytics or behavioral data (via external services)
- Payment information

## 4. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to display statistics and generate analysis reports within the App. It is never shared with third parties.

## 5. Camera, Microphone, and Photo Library Access

The App requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Camera | To record video of matches and practice sessions |
| Microphone | To capture audio during video recording |
| Photo Library | To select previously recorded videos from your library |

These permissions are only used when explicitly granted by the user. Video data obtained through these permissions is never transmitted externally.

## 6. Data Storage and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App or using the "Reset Data" function in the Settings screen.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ but does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Significant changes will be communicated through App update release notes.
